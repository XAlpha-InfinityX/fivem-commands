LSPDFR Commands List

  • Basic LSPDFR keybinds

    • • F4 - Console menu (opens/closes console menu)
    • • B - Backup (opens/closes backup menu)
    • • B - Crime report (When spotting a crime, hold button until bar is full to report a crime)
    • • N - Police car/pursuit menu (opens/closes the police car/pursuit menu)
    • • M / hold or - Interaction menu (opens/closes interaction menu)
    • • E - Cuff (hold E while standing above a ped to cuff them)
    • • Q - Frisk suspect (frisks a suspect)
    • • E - Make suspect sit (press E while ped cuffed to make them sit)
    • • Y - Answer/accept callout (accepts a callout)
    • • Left shift - Start traffic stop (Tags a vehicle to attempt to stop them)
    • • J - Toggle siren (toggles siren, do this behind tagged vehicle to attempt to pull them over)
    • • Q - Police computer (opens the police computer)

  • LSPFR arrests and interactions

    • • Aim at ped and press E / hold - or Gunpoint takedown (starts a progress bar which when filled, the suspect will comply and get down where you can then cuff them)
    • • While behind ped, press Q / press - or Arrested ped stop following (makes arrested ped stop following you)
    • • While behind ped, press E / press - or Take hold of arrested ped (takes hold of arrested suspect in order to move them)
    • • Aim at ped and hold Q / hold - or Pat-down frisk (performs pat-down frisk of arrested suspect)
    • • While behind ped, hold E / hold - or Release arrested suspect (release the arrested suspect)
    • • While holding ped near rear doors, hold E / hold - or Detain in police cruiser (puts arrested ped in police cruiser)
    • • While facing door of vehicle, press E / press - or Open door of cruiser (opens the door you're facing of the police cruiser)
    • • While facing open door of vehicle, press E / press - or Tell suspect to get in vehicle (makes the suspect get in the vehicle on their own)
    • • While door open and ped inside, press E / press - or Remove suspect from cruiser (removes the detained ped from the police cruiser)

  • Transporting arrested peds

    • • Transport a ped to jail yourself by going to any of the jail symbols on your map
    • • Stand behind arrested ped and press Q / press - or Suspect sit on ground (makes the arrested suspect sit on the ground)
    • • When suspect sitting on ground, and press B for backup menu and call a transport unit with response type of transport - Request prisoner transport (requests a prisoner transport unit)

  • LSPDFR Apartments

    • • Pause menu - Allows access to your apartments (LSPDFR adds 4 apartments which contain clothes, food, and your garage where you can drive a vehicle to save to. Note: You can also use interaction menu to set waypoints to your apartments)

  • LSPFR balistic shield

    • • M / or - Using interaction menu to equip balistic shield (By equiping a balistic shield you can be partially protected from bullets and other projectiles)

  • LSPFR evidence marker

    • • M / or - Using interaction menu to put down evidence marker (You can put evidence markers down, and pick them up by walking towards them and following prompts)

  • LSPFR interaction menu

    • • Quick GPS lets you quickly set a GPS waypoint to the nearest police station, jail drop-off, fire station, medical center, or your apartment.
    • • Police Radio is only available while on duty and brings you to the Police Radio sub menu. See Police Computer & Radio for more info.
    • • Vehicle brings you to a sub menu that gives you access to some of your vehicle's functions.
    • • Inventory brings you to another sub menu with some options relating to your character's inventory.
    • • Settings is yet another sub menu with some options for LSPDFR.
    • • Action lets you perform a few different quick actions, such as drinking coffee or eating donuts. Press Enter to perform the action.
    • • Dialogue lets you say various things like "Hi" or "Bye." Press Enter to say it.
    • • Player mood changes your character's facial expression.
    • • Walk style changes the animation your character uses for walking.
    • • The Illuminated Clothing option allows you to control the style of any illuminated (emissive) clothing on your character's outfit.
